## 4. LEGAL AND OPERATIONAL REDLINES

1. **PRICE ERROR REDLINE**: PRICE_ERROR_REVIEW_REQUIRED. ORDER_MAY_BE_SUSPENDED. MANUAL_LEGAL_DECISION_REQUIRED. WRITTEN_AVOIDANCE_DECLARATION_WHERE_APPLICABLE. CUSTOMER_NOTIFICATION_REQUIRED. AUDIT_TRAIL_REQUIRED.
2. **JDG / CONSUMER-LIKE PROTECTION REDLINE**: Must distinguish pure B2B from sole traders acting out of professional character (Art. 7aa Consumer Rights Act, Art. 385(5) KC). Statutory withdrawal rights and warranty must be addressed appropriately.
3. **DATA RETENTION REDLINE**: RETENTION_POLICY=MATRIX_BY_DATA_CATEGORY. PERSONAL_OPERATIONAL_LOGS=TIME_LIMITED. SECURITY_LOGS=TIME_LIMITED_WITH_PERIODIC_REVIEW. ACCOUNTING_RECORDS=STATUTORY_RETENTION_SUBJECT_TO_TAX_CONFIRMATION. KSEF_INVOICES=KSEF_STATUTORY_RETENTION_10_YEARS. LEGAL_HOLD=SUPPORTED. ANONYMISATION_AFTER_RETENTION=REQUIRED_WHERE_APPLICABLE. DELETION_OR_ANONYMISATION_JOB=FUTURE_IMPLEMENTATION. RETENTION_PERIODS_REQUIRE_FORMAL_APPROVAL=YES.
4. **PAYMENT SERVICES / KNF REDLINE**: LEG_GATE_10_MODEL_STATUS=MODEL_A_APPROVED_CONDITIONALLY. CONDITION_1=LOGIMARKET_IS_CONTRACTUAL_SELLER. CONDITION_2=PAYMENT_DISCHARGES_BUYER_DEBT_TO_LOGIMARKET. CONDITION_3=SUPPLIER_HAS_NO_DIRECT_PAYMENT_CLAIM_TO_BUYER. CONDITION_4=SUPPLIER_PAYMENT_IS_SEPARATE_TRADE_PAYABLE. CONDITION_5=PSP_ACCEPTS_THE_MODEL. CONDITION_6=CONTRACTS_AND_ACTUAL_OPERATIONS_MATCH_THE_MODEL. FORMAL_LEGAL_CONFIRMATION=PENDING. PSP_CONFIRMATION=PENDING.
5. **KSeF REQUIREMENTS**: The current official KSeF rollout must be treated as a current compliance requirement. KSEF_INVOICE_ID, KSEF_REFERENCE_NUMBER, KSEF_STATUS, KSEF_SUBMISSION_MODE, KSEF_SUBMITTED_AT, KSEF_ACCEPTED_AT, KSEF_REJECTED_AT, KSEF_ERROR_CODE, KSEF_ERROR_MESSAGE, KSEF_INVOICE_VERSION, INVOICE_CORRECTION_RELATION, CORRECTED_INVOICE_ID, KSEF_OFFLINE_OR_FAILURE_MODE, KSEF_QR_OR_VERIFICATION_REFERENCE, INVOICE_ISSUER_SNAPSHOT, CUSTOMER_TAX_ID_SNAPSHOT, SUPPLIER_TAX_ID_SNAPSHOT are logical requirements for LM-DROP-DATA-MODEL-56B0.
6. **POST CAPTURE CANCELLATION FLOW**: Operational cancellation remains supported after payment capture. Buyer-initiated cancellation rules and operational cancellation are separate concepts.
7. **FREIGHT OFFER MODEL INVARIANT**: OFFER_MODEL_IMPACT=NO_AUTOMATIC_OFFER_MODEL_CHANGE. Oversized or project products already using offerModel=rfq remain RFQ. An ecommerce offer must never be converted automatically to RFQ because freight is unsupported.
